About the App

Layers of Knowledge is an intelligent spaced repetition app built on the science of the Ebbinghaus Forgetting Curve. Transform how you remember information with perfectly timed review reminders.

Studies show we forget up to 70% of new information within 24 hours. Our scientifically optimized schedule fights this by prompting reviews at proven intervals.

Day 1 Day 3 Day 7 Day 14 Day 30 Day 90 Day 180

Frequently Asked Questions

How does spaced repetition work?

Spaced repetition shows you information at increasing intervals (1, 3, 7, 14, 30, 90, and 180 days). This timing is optimized based on the Ebbinghaus Forgetting Curve to help transfer knowledge from short-term to long-term memory.

Can I create my own review schedule?

Yes! While we provide scientifically-backed preset schedules, you can create custom schedules with your own intervals. Define exactly when you want to be reminded – whether it's days, weeks, or months apart. Each schedule can have unlimited layers.

What happens when I pause notifications?

When you pause, all notifications are suspended. When you resume, your pending reviews are automatically rescheduled from that moment, preserving your progress without losing any items.

How do I transfer my data to a new device?

Use the Export feature in Settings to download your entire knowledge base as a JSON file. On your new device, use the Import feature to restore all your items, tags, and schedules.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All your data stays on your device. We don't require an account, don't collect any personal information, and don't have access to your knowledge items.

Can I relearn something I've already completed?

Yes! Use the Relearn feature to restart any item's review cycle from the beginning. This is perfect for information that needs reinforcement or topics you want to revisit.

What languages are supported?

Layers of Knowledge fully supports English, French, and Arabic content, including proper right-to-left text handling for Arabic and diacritics-aware search for French characters.
